BREED: RAGDOLL CAT

WEIGHT: UP TO 20 POUNDS

LIFESPAN: UP TO 17 YEARS

PERSONALITY: FRIENDLY EASY-GOING CUDDLY AND SOCIAL

EYE COLOR: BLUE

CHARACTERISTICS: The big, beautiful ragdoll cat does not reach its full size, about 15 pounds on average, until about four years of age. With its easygoing personality and social temperament, the ragdoll is ready to be friends with just about anyone.
